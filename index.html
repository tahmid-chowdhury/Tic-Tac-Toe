Tic-Tac-Toe

HOW TO PLAY: Click on a square to place an 'X' on the board. The computer will place 'O's. Get three in a row to win!

This game is written in JavaScript is played against the computer. The minimax algorithm is used to determine the best move for the computer player.

Created by Tahmid Chowdhury, Armaghan Nasir, and Sharujan Sivanandam.
